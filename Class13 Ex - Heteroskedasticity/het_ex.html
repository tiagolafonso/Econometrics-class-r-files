  1. After estimating the model in (a), obtain the residuals, \(\color{purple}{\hat{\mu}_i}\). Construct a scatter plot of the squared residuals (\(\color{purple}{\hat{\mu}_i^2}\)) against each of the explanatory variables (lotsize and bdrms). Do these plots visually suggest the presence of heteroscedasticity?

  2. Perform the Breusch-Pagan test for heteroscedasticity for the previous equation. Based on this result, what do you conclude about the presence of heteroscedasticity?”

\[ \color{purple}{price = \beta_0 + \beta_1 lotsize + \beta_2 bdrms + \mu_i} \]

Discuss any important differences with the usual standard errors.
